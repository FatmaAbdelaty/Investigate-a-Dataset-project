Introduction¶

It is an investigation of a dataset of No show appointments to analyze data to make sure the patient will attend at its appointment or not.

Dataset Description¶

This dataset collects information from 100k medical appointments in Brazil. It want be to determine whether or not patients show up for their appointment. characteristics of each patient are included in each row.

1- 'ScheduledDay' tells us on what day the patient set up their appointment. 2-'Neighborhood' indicates the location of the hospital. 3-'Scholarship' indicates whether or not the patient is enrolled in Brasilian welfare program Bolsa Famlia. 4-'No_show' it says 'No' if the patient showed up to their appointment, and 'Yes' if they did not show up.

Question(s) for Analysis¶

What factors are important for us to know in order to predict if a patient will show up for their scheduled appointment?

def fact_show (df ,c_name,show,noshow):
    plt.figure(figsize=[16,4])
    df[c_name][show].hist(alpha=0.5, bins=20, label='Show')
    df[c_name][noshow].hist(alpha=0.5, bins=20, label='noShow')
    plt.legend();
    plt.title('Comparison between who show and no show according to age ')
    plt.xlabel("Patients' Age ")
    plt.ylabel('Num of patients');
